Join us in making a difference by bridging the gap between surplas food and those in need. In the Karamoja region, over 518,000 people are facing critical food insecurity, with 90,000 at emergency levels without food to eat. Our app empowers individuals and businesses to easily donate excess food, ensuring it reaches local food banks and shelters swiftly. Together, we can fight famine, reduce waste, and build stronger communities. Ready to make an impact? Get started today and be a part of the solution.
